Pandemic Powers: Tech's Opportunities and Challenges

The pandemic landscape is an exotic and strange one, bubbling with new opportunities and hidden pitfalls. Regulators and lawmakers who in years past adopted a hands-off policy toward tech are more closely scrutinizing every move made in Seattle and Silicon Valley. The SEC is ramping up to take a great look at the SPAC boom. And while a new exuberance has followed the pandemic, there are questions about just how complete the recovery will be. Join us as we explore the opportunities and challenges at the Bloomberg Technology Summit, 2021.
